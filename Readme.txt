Project Title - MIND MORPHERS 

	It is a quiz game based on LCU(Lokesh Cinematic Universe) movies.
	Your score will be display as LCU characters.

About the project:
	Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
	Backend:  JavaScript.

To this project:
	
	1.Open Mind Morphers.html file.
	2.Click "LETS START" button.
	3.Then, you have enter your name,then click "START QUIZ" button.
	4.Now,quiz will be started.
	5.You have to attend 10 questions which is asked based on LCU movies.
	6.At end of the quiz, your name, score & LCU character (based on your score) will be shown.
